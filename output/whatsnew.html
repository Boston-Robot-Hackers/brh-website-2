Montly Meetings

As we are still a very new club we are still figuring out how our meetings work. Here is our current approach. We meet about once a month, around the first Thursday of the month. The meetings are always on a Thursday from 7:00pm to 9:00pm. Our meetings happen at the Artisans Asylum in Boston (Alston) Massachusetts.
